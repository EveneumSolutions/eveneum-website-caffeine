# Eveneum Solutions Company Website

## Overview
A professional company website for Eveneum Solutions, a staffing and recruitment firm. The website showcases the company's services, provides information for both companies and candidates, and includes a contact form for inquiries.

## Pages and Navigation
The website includes the following pages accessible through main navigation:
- Home
- For Companies  
- For Candidates
- About Us
- Services
- Industries
- Blog
- Contact
